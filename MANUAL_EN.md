# Digital Fortuneteller User Manual

Welcome to 12 Divinations - your personal application for exploring ancient and modern divination methods. Discover the wisdom of the ages and get answers to your questions with our intuitive interface.

## Introduction

12 Divinations is a web application that brings together 12 unique divination methods from around the world. Each method is presented as an interactive component, allowing you to immerse yourself in the world of predictions and gain new insights into your life situation. The application is designed for entertainment, self-discovery, and inspiration.

## Available Divination Methods

The application features the following types of divination:

### 1. Fortune Cookie
A simple and beloved way to get a short and wise piece of advice. Just "break" the digital cookie and read your fortune.

### 2. I Ching (Book of Changes)
An ancient Chinese divination system based on 64 hexagrams. Ask a question and receive a deep philosophical answer that will help you analyze the situation.

### 3. Runic Divination
Explore the wisdom of the ancient Scandinavian runes. Each rune carries a unique meaning and can provide a clue about your path.

### 4. Crystal Ball
A classic tool for predictions. Concentrate on your question, and the crystal ball will show you a symbolic image or answer.

### 5. Egyptian Pyramids
Unlock the secrets of Ancient Egypt. This divination method uses the symbolism of the pyramids to provide unique predictions related to your destiny.

### 6. Greco-Roman Oracles
Receive advice from the ancient gods and oracles. This method draws inspiration from the wisdom of classical antiquity.

### 7. Druid Divination
Connect with nature and the ancient wisdom of the Celtic priests. Druid divination will help you find harmony and balance.

### 8. Caribbean Dice
A dynamic and colorful divination method from the Caribbean islands. Roll the dice to find out what fate has in store for you.

### 9. Korean Pendulum
A traditional Asian method that uses a pendulum to get "yes" or "no" answers and other indications.

### 10. Mayan Glyphs
Decipher the mysterious symbols of the Mayan civilization. Each glyph reveals an aspect of your personality or future.

### 11. Slavic Kostromancy
An ancient Slavic divination associated with fire and symbols. Learn your destiny through images born in the flames.

### 12. Thai Figurines
A unique method based on the symbolism of traditional Thai figurines. Choose a figurine and find out its meaning for your situation.

## How to use the application

Using Digital Fortuneteller is very simple:

1.  **Choose a divination method:** On the main screen, you will see a list of available methods. Click on the one that interests you.
2.  **Interact with the component:** Follow the instructions on the screen. This could be pressing a button, selecting a symbol, or another interactive element.
3.  **Get your prediction:** Your result will be displayed on the screen.

## Localization

The application supports two languages: English and Russian. You can switch between languages using the corresponding button in the interface.
